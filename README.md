## Selected case studies

### International market launch — Omaze (Germany)
**Role:** Lead engineer, end-to-end. **Team:** 1 other engineer + product designer + external Shopify agency. **Timeline:** under 3 months from kickoff to live market.

What this looked like in practice:
- Shopify front-end integrated with KYC, support tooling, Stripe, Recharge, and an AWS Lambda / Node.js draw + fulfilment backend.
- Region-specific compliance: data residency, KYC providers, deletion flows.
- Analytics and observability through PostHog and a NoSQL data warehouse.
- Vendor coordination across the Shopify agency, payment partners, and KYC vendors — all running while the internal team stayed lean.

**Why it mattered:** the kind of launch that usually justifies a 10-engineer programme team got delivered by 2 people + a partner. End result: a live, transacting market in a region with strict compliance requirements.
